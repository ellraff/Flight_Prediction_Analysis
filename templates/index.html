Predict Your Delays

Unsure your layover is long enough?

Or if you'll get to that event on time?

Flights are always an issue especially with layovers and airlines always try to make extremely short layover, some of which give very little wiggle room for delays, which happen all the time. This model povides a way to see whether your flight might be early, only delayed up to 15 minutes, or over 30 minutese, so you can gauge whether the layover time will be enough for you to disembark and run across the terminal to the next flight. Of course, there are always extreme curcumstances and this year has been especially inconsistent, but this model is built from data since September 2020.

The model uses flight data for incoming and outgoing California flights and was gathered from the Bureau of Transportation Website created by the United Stated Department of Transportation.
